About Raudranex

Pioneering the Future of In-Space Propulsion

Our Mission

At Raudranex Space Technologies, we are developing next-generation electric propulsion systems that make deep-space exploration practical, scalable, and sustainable. Our mission is to revolutionize satellite and spacecraft mobility, enabling humanity's ambitious journey into the cosmos.

Who We Are

Raudranex is a deep-tech space startup founded by a team of aerospace engineers and researchers passionate about advancing propulsion technology. We combine cutting-edge research with practical engineering to develop propulsion systems that meet the demands of modern space missions.

Our focus is on electric propulsionspecifically Arcjet and Ion thruster technologieswhich offer superior performance metrics including high specific impulse, fuel efficiency, and reliability for satellite constellation deployments, orbit corrections, and interplanetary missions.

Our Technology

MAYUKHA - AF-MPD Thruster

Our advanced Arcjet thruster delivering exceptional thrust and specific impulse, ideal for primary propulsion in satellite applications.

  • Thrust: 70-95 mN
  • Specific Impulse: 1300-1400s
  • Propellant: Argon/Xenon/Hydrogen
  • Power: 1.0 kW

MIHIRA - RF-Gridded Ion Thruster

Our precision ion thruster optimized for fine orbit adjustments and long-duration missions with minimal propellant consumption.

  • Thrust: 20-35 mN
  • Specific Impulse: ~1050s
  • Propellant: Air/Nitrogen
  • Power: 400 W
